Project You

Project You is a project trying to emphasise the increasingly important role of technology in design and art fields. I came up the idea by thinking about an installation that can bring the latest technologies together and interact with audiences directly. Because interaction is apparently a big part that technology can make arts and design different from what they used to be. With the ability of width and depth scanning of Xbox Kinect sensor, I can capture three-dimensional models for the participants of this project and print them out with a 3D printer.
